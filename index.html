Some Cooking terms to know!

Sautee
fried quickly in a little hot fat
Blanched
(of an item of food) having been briefly immersed in boiling water, especially in order to remove the skin or to prepare it for further cooking.
Baste
To moisten food while it is cooking in order to keep it from drying out.
